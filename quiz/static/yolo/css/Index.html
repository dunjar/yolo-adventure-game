The game is played by two players at the same time.

First, you will pick your name and avatar and wait for another player to join you or join another player, who is already waiting for you.

When the game begins, you will see a question or a described situation. You have to pick the right thing to do in that situation or the right answer by klicking on the correct box. You will have 1 minute to answer each question.

After both players answer the question or after one minute has passed, you will see your score, and that of your opponent.

Player with more points is the winner of the game.

Have fun and don't forget to wash your hands with soap and warm water, because - YOLO!
